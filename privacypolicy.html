Global Track Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 28, 2025**

Welcome to Global Track. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights concerning that information.

1. Information We Collect

We are a non-affiliated, third-party package tracking service. Our primary function is to process a tracking number and return the shipment status.

Information Collected from You:

  • **Tracking Numbers:** When you enter a tracking number (e.g., **USPS tracking number**, **FedEx tracking number**), we temporarily use this number to retrieve data from the respective shipping carrier's API. We **do not** store your tracking numbers on our servers after the status is delivered to you.
  • **Non-Personal Information:** We may collect non-identifiable data such as browser type, operating system, and the time of your visit to help us improve our service.

We DO NOT collect or store Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, physical addresses, or payment information.

2. Use of Tracking Data

The only purpose for processing the tracking number is to retrieve the current status of your package. The package status information is displayed to you and is not used for any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your non-personal usage information to outside parties.

  • **Shipping Carriers:** To provide the tracking service, we must transmit your tracking number to the relevant shipping carrier (e.g., **United States Postal Service**, **UPS**, **DHL**) via their secure API. Their use of this data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use minimal session cookies to maintain the functionality of your visit. These cookies are not used to track your personal activities across other websites.

5. Your Consent

By using our site and service, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

6. Contacting Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us through the Support link provided on the main website.

This site uses cookies to enhance user experience. By continuing to use the site, you agree to our Privacy Policy.
